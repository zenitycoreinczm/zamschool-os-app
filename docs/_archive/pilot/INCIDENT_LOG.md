## Severity

| Level | Meaning | Target response |
|-------|---------|-----------------|
| **P1** | Production down or data integrity risk | 15 min acknowledge, 4h mitigate |
| **P2** | Major feature broken for many users | Same business day |
| **P3** | Minor / workaround exists | Next sprint |
| **P4** | Cosmetic / doc | Backlog |
